About me


Hi, Oliver here,
and thanks for checking out my Portfolio. I am a self-taught web developer based in London / Surrey. Focused mainly on the front-end side of things, my developement has come a long way; from being a comeplete beginner, to being competent and confident at making user friendly projects with easy on the eye aesthetics.

As I said above, I'm a self taught programmer, so it's been a long, and at times challenging journey, but above all it's been one of the most enjoyable and rewarding quests I have had the pleasure to embark on.

My journey started just like everyone else's, with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. At this stage I am very comfortable in my javascript, and have now added React to my repotoire. It's still early days with React but I am enjoying the next step in advancing my capabilities.

Below I have a few pieces of work I have produced, please feel free to check them out (and the code that has produced them) and let me know what you think.

The projects range from large to small, with the bigger ones being mock website ideas, to smaller CSS styles and animation. And with some early learning Javascript in the middle as well.
